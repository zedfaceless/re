How-To Repair Guides

Changing Your Oil

Step-by-step guide on how to change your oil...

1. Prepare the tools and oil.

2. Locate the oil drain plug and oil filter.

3. Drain the oil and replace the filter.

4. Refill with new oil and check levels.

Brake Pad Replacement

Learn how to replace your brake pads...

1. Loosen the lug nuts and raise the vehicle.

2. Remove the caliper and old brake pads.

3. Install new brake pads and reassemble.

4. Test the brakes for proper installation.

Replacing the Battery

How to safely replace your vehicle's battery...

1. Turn off the engine and remove keys.

2. Disconnect the negative and positive terminals.

3. Remove the old battery and clean terminals.

4. Install the new battery and reconnect terminals.

Replacing Wiper Blades

Step-by-step guide on replacing wiper blades...

1. Lift the wiper arm and remove old blades.

2. Attach the new wiper blades securely.

3. Lower the wiper arm carefully.

Checking Tire Pressure

Maintain proper tire pressure with this guide...

1. Find the recommended PSI for your tires.

2. Use a tire gauge to check pressure.

3. Inflate or deflate as necessary.

Headlight Bulb Replacement

Instructions for replacing a headlight bulb...

1. Open the hood and locate the bulb housing.

2. Remove the old bulb and insert a new one.

3. Test the headlights to ensure functionality.

Replacing Air Filter

Guide on changing the engine air filter...

1. Locate and open the air filter housing.

2. Remove the old air filter and replace it.
